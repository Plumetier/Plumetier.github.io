About Me

My Name is Priyam Biswas

 pronunciation /Pre.yum: Bis.vas/

I’m a 25-year-old 3D generalist with a deep passion for gaming, based in Eastern Canada. 3D is my lifeblood.

My goal is to bring a smile to my audience with every project I tackle.

I specialize in 3D Modeling, Texturing, Rigging, and Animation, crafting finely detailed and optimized assets for game engines.

Additionally, I've refined my abilities to render product shots, and hero assets using tools like Maya, Blender, Substance, and Unreal Engine.

I also enjoy experimenting with Gaea, Speedtree, and Unity to create immersive environments.
